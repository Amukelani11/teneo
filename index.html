 Term 3 = rewards season for ALL learners!

Earn up to R800 in Takealot vouchers this term by collecting points on the Points Leaderboard.

Mandela Day Challenge: Every Minute Counts!

This Mandela Day, we encourage all Teneo learners to complete at least 67 minutes of community service hours during the July holidays to support meaningful causes.
 Your challenge: Complete at least 67 minutes of community service during the July holidays.
How to get involved:

  Volunteer at an animal shelter
  Help deliver food to those in need
  Join a clean-up or plant trees
  Tutor or read with someone
  Visit a care home
  Create care packs or support a local clinic
 Record your impact in 3 easy steps:

 

1. Take a photo of yourself completing your service.

2. Upload it to the Nelson Mandela Day Community Service Quiz in your Social Connect course on Canvas.

3. Log your minutes and activity using the form in the quiz.
